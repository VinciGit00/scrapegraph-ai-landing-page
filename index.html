What is ScrapegraphAI?

ScrapeGraphAI is our popular open-source library designed for efficient web data extraction. It enables developers to scrape and extract structured information from websites using advanced AI techniques. Due to its success and high demand, we are building a SaaS platform around it to make this powerful tool even more accessible to non-developers.

Who can benefit afrom using ScrapegraphAI?

ScrapegraphAI and our SaaS products (ScrapeLink and SearchGraph) are ideal for businesses, researchers, data analysts, and marketers who need to extract structured data from the web. Whether youâ€™re gathering data from specific URLs or performing searches to gather insights, our tools simplify the process and deliver results quickly.

Which websites are supported?

ScrapeGraphAI is best suited for extracting data from a wide variety of websites, including business websites, documentation pages, help centers, e-commerce product listings, blogs, news articles, educational content, and more. We currently do not support websites that require authentication.

What are ScrapeLink and SearchGraph?

- ScrapeLink is our tool that allows you to extract data from a specific list of URLs. Simply provide a list of URLs, input a prompt describing the data you're looking for, and define a schema to structure the output. ScrapeLink will process the URLs and return the requested information in a spreadsheet format.

- SearchGraph, on the other hand, takes web data extraction a step further by automating the search process. Instead of providing URLs, you simply enter a prompt and define the schema for the output. SearchGraph will use a search engine to find the top 5 relevant webpages based on your prompt and extract the requested data from those pages. Each search performed by SearchGraph is equivalent to processing 5 webpages.

Does ScrapeGraphAI handle proxy rotation, rate limits etc?

Yes, we take care of challenges like proxy rotation, rate limits, and other technical obstacles, making your data extraction process smooth and hassle-free. Our goal is to simplify the process so you can focus on getting the data you need without worrying about these complexities.

How does pricing work?

We charge based on the number of webpages processed. Each URL you submit in ScrapeLink will cost 1 webpage, while each search conducted by SearchGraph will cost 5 webpages (as multiple URLs are fetched automatically). There are no complicated credits or tokens, just straightforward billing based on the number of webpages you process!

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through Stripe, supporting most major credit cards, debit cards and PayPal, ensuring a convenient payment experience.

Do I get charged for failed requests?

No, never! We do not charge for any failed requests (ScrapeLink or SearchGraph). Please contact us at support@scrapegraphai.com if you have any questions.
